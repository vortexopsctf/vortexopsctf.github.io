About CTF Events

What is a CTF?

Capture The Flag (CTF) competitions are cybersecurity challenges where participants solve security-related tasks to find hidden "flags" and earn points.

Event Formats

Jeopardy: Solve challenges in various categories
Attack-Defense: Defend your services while attacking others
Mixed: Combination of both formats

How to Participate

Click on any event to visit its CTFtime page. Register through the official event website, join our team, and start solving challenges!
